Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake? expand_more
For bespoke creations, we recommend placing your order at least 3 weeks in advance. During peak wedding season (May-September), 6-8 weeks is preferred to ensure we can dedicate the necessary time to craft your perfect piece.
Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options? expand_more
Yes, we have a curated selection of gluten-free and vegan pastries available daily. Custom cakes can also be adapted to meet these dietary requirements without compromising our signature taste and texture.
What are your opening hours? expand_more
Our flagship patisserie is open Tuesday through Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sundays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on Mondays to prepare for the week ahead.
